A Complete List of Things & Places Hyderabad Is Famous For ...Hyderabad is famous for its rich Nizami history, iconic landmarks like the Charminar and Golconda Fort, its unique fusion of cultures, the world-renowned Hyderabadi biryani, and its historical status as the "City of Pearls" for its diamond and pearl trade. It's also a modern hub for the booming IT industry and the Telugu film industry (Tollywood). Historical & Cultural Landmarks Charminar: The city's symbol, built in 1591, with its mosque and prayer halls.
